                '''Brando Franco Windah''' (born 14 March 1992), better known by his online alias ''' Windah Basudara''' or simply as''' Brando,''' is an Indonesian [[YouTuber]] and [[online streamer]], best known for his [[video game live streaming]] and [[Let's Play]] videos on [[YouTube]] and noted for his comedic persona, he is also the brand ambassador for the Indonesian based esport organization [[Rex Regum Qeon]] since 2021.{{cite news |last=Priyambodo |first=Apriliandi Damar |editor-last=Basudewa |editor-first=Gangga |title=Windah Basudara Resmi Jadi Brand Ambassador Team RRQ |language=id |trans-title=Windah Basudara Officially Becomes RRQ Team's Brand Ambassador |url=https://esports.skor.id/windah-basudara-resmi-jadi-brand-ambassador-team-rrq-01381658 |publisher=Skor.id |date=9 June 2021 |access-date=3 January 2022}}{{cite news |last1=Oktarini |first1=Dinar Surya |last2=Prisilia |first2=Amelia |date=3 July 2021|title=Jadi Brand Ambassador RRQ, Windah Basudara Mabar dengan Lemon dan Xin |language=id |trans-title=Windah Basudara Becomes RRQ Brand Ambassador with Lemon and Xin |publisher=Voice |url=https://www.suara.com/tekno/2021/07/03/190748/jadi-brand-ambassador-rrq-windah-basudara-mabar-dengan-lemon-dan-xin |access-date=28 December 2021}}
                
                ==Early life==
                Brando Franco Windah was born on 14 March 1996 in [[Manado]], [[North Sulawesi]], he is the second child of 4 siblings. He has two sisters, Florence and Caroline, and a younger brother, Vincent.{{cite news |date=3 March 2021 |title=Profil Windah Basudara, YouTuber Gaming yang Punya Jutaan Subscriber |language=id |trans-title=Profile of Windah Basudara, a Gaming YouTuber with Millions of Subscribers |publisher=[[:id:Kumparan (situs web)|Kumparan]] |url=https://kumparan.com/berita-hari-ini/profil-windah-basudara-youtuber-gaming-yang-punya-jutaan-subscriber-1vHe49j3nmB/2 |access-date=28 December 2021}} Brando and his family moved and settle in [[Jakarta]] when he was still in Kindergarten, and grew up there. He attended [[:ms:Sekolah Menengah Pertama St. Vincentius|SD Santo Vincentius]] elementary, [[:id:SMP Marsudirini Maria Immaculata Jakarta|SMP Marsudirini]] junior high and [[:id:Yayasan Perguruan Santo Bellarminus|SMA Santo Bellarminus]] high school.{{cite news |last=Andreas |first=Fernando |date=23 March 2022 |title=Profil Windah Basudara, YouTuber Gaming yang Miliki Jutaan Subscriber |language=id |trans-title=Profile of Windah Basudara, a Gaming YouTuber Who Has Millions of Subscribers |publisher=[[Blitz (newspaper)|Blitz]] |url=https://www.blitsnews.com/news/pr-2593020362/profil-windah-basudara-youtuber-gaming-yang-miliki-jutaan-subscriber |access-date=5 June 2022 |archive-date=9 October 2022 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20221009060616/https://www.blitsnews.com/news/pr-2593020362/profil-windah-basudara-youtuber-gaming-yang-miliki-jutaan-subscriber |url-status=dead }}{{cite news |title=Profil Windah Basudara, YouTuber Gaming dengan Hampir 8 Juta Subscriber |publisher= [[Kompas]] |url=https://entertainment.kompas.com/read/2022/07/17/120719066/profil-windah-basudara-youtuber-gaming-dengan-hampir-8-juta-subscriber |access-date=17 July 2022 |date=17 July 2022}}{{cite news |title=Siapa Windah Basudara? Jadi Trending Gegara Akun YouTube Miliknya Kena Hack |url=https://www.suara.com/lifestyle/2022/07/16/185507/siapa-windah-basudara-jadi-trending-gegara-akun-youtube-miliknya-kena-hack |access-date=17 July 2022 |publisher=Suara.com |date=16 July 2022}}
                
                When Windah was in the [[eleventh grade]], he was frequently [[bullying|bullied]] by his [[Class (education)|classmates]], eventually leading him to quit school and work for his father as an [[internet café]] operator. His parents convinced him to continue his education to graduate with a [[diploma]], so he later applied for a [[homeschooling]] program run by [[:id:Seto Mulyadi|Kak Seto]],{{cite news |last=Syahdan |first=Oleh |date=13 January 2021 |title=[PROFILE] Getting to Know Windah Basudara's Brando, Pre-Teens' Favorite YouTuber |publisher=Dunia Games |agency=Telkomsel |url=https://duniagames.co.id/discover/article/profil-brando-windah-basudara/en |access-date=3 January 2022}} an Indonesian [[Psychologist]].
                
                After graduating from high school, Windah went to college where he studied [[International relations]] for a [[Bachelor's degree]] (Strata-1 in Indonesian academic standard) at [[:id:STIKOM The London School of Public Relations|The London School of Public Relations]] (LSPR), but he had to drop out due to economic constraints.
                
                In July 2022, his channel is hacked by a live streaming video showing an American businessman named [[Brad Garlinghouse]]https://www.tribunnewswiki.com/2022/07/16/kanal-youtubenya-diduga-kena-hack-windah-basudara-ingatkan-hal-ini-ke-subscriber
                
                After quitting college, Windah began his working life as a [[waiter]] in a small café in Jakarta, where he earned around [[Indonesian rupiah|IDR]] 900,000 per month. He quit after only a month due to the low wage. Soon after, Windah started work at the Bluegrass restaurant in [[South Jakarta]], where he was trained to be more professional, so his communication skills improved rapidly.
                
                After three months at the Bluegrass, hotel officials from ''The Westin Jakarta Hotel'' visited the restaurant and Windah received an offer of work at the new [[five-star hotel]] due to open in the [[Gama Tower]]. Having decided to accept the offer, Windah quit his job at the restaurant. Because it was several months before ''The Westin Jakarta'' would open, he was unable to start immediately, so in the meantime, Windah had to find work in another restaurant.
                
                Once working at the hotel, Windah earned a salary of around IDR 10,000,000. After approximately two and a half years working in the five-star hotel, Windah felt he was being passed over for promotion and decided that he did not want to remain in the same position any longer, so he resigned. He met his girlfriend Sesilia Agnes while still working at the hotel.
                
                Windah decided working at a hotel was not where he wanted to be, he saw his future as a professional YouTuber, particularly in the [[gamer|gaming category]].
                
                ===Personal life===
                When Windah was in his [[freshman year]] in [[middle school]], he experienced bullying by his schoolmates, this could have been due to his tall, lanky stature. He did not tell his parents because he felt ashamed to admit that others saw him this way. The bullying continued into high school which is why he decided to quit in second grade.
                
                ==Career==
                Windah had been fond of playing video games since childhood. The first [[game console|console]] he played on was a [[Nintendo Entertainment System]]. Since beginning elementary school, Windah was fond of his [[PlayStation 1]] (PSX) and the RPG game [[Suikoden II]].
                
                Windah's interest in gaming continued into adulthood, although at that time he was working as a waiter in a five-star hotel. After he saw the success of YouTubers like [[:id:MiawAug|MiawAug]], he felt he had the potential to become a professional Gaming YouTuber and decided to resign from the hotel.{{cite news |last=Puspitasari |first=Nisa |date=4 February 2021 |title=Biodata Windah Basudara, Lengkap Umur dan Agama, Gamer Banyak Genre Favorit Milenial dan Gen Z |language=id |trans-title=Windah Basudara Biodata, Complete Age and Religion, Gamers Many Millennials and Gen Z Favorite Genres |publisher=Kuyou |url=https://kuyou.id/homepage/read/18769/biodata-windah-basudara-lengkap-umur-dan-agama-gamer-banyak-genre-favorit-milenial-dan-gen-z |access-date=5 June 2022}} To begin his new career, he bought an [[Asus#Republic of Gamers (ROG)|Asus ROG]] laptop for IDR 13,000,000, but he was still nervous about starting his own channel, so he planned to invite two of his siblings, Caroline and Vincent, so each could start their own channel at the same time. However, because they were still at school, he had no choice but to build a channel by himself. He created the channel Windah Basudara, taken from the [[family name]] Windah, and ''basudara'', which means 'siblings' in [[Manado Malay|Manado language]]. Despite being named ''basudara'', his siblings have never featured in the channel. His online alias is a [[misnomer]] as the term ''basudara'' refers to Windah and his siblings.
                
                ===YouTube career===
                Windah Basudara started his YouTube career on 29 Dec 2018. To kick-off his YouTuber career, the laptop which Windah had paid Rp. 13 million for a month earlier was damaged, so it had to be taken in for repair. Now desperate, he tried a return to hotel work, but each application was rejected. Windah confided in his mother, who offered him a loan so he could buy a new [[Personal computer|PC]]. He revealed that once he had difficulties getting viewers, so he began adding spam links in chats on several social media platforms such as [[Facebook]], [[Google+]] and [[Line (software)|Line Square]].{{cite news |last=Syahdan |first=Oleh |date=24 May 2021 |title=Windah Basudara Shares Hardship of Getting Viewers Before His Breakthrough |publisher=Dunia Games |agency=Telkomsel |url=https://duniagames.co.id/discover/article/windah-basudara-curhat-soal-sulitnya-mendapatkan-penonton/en |access-date=5 June 2022}} 
                
                Due to his massive success as a streamer, he now amasses around 100k viewers during his live streams. In April 2019, Windah Basudara only had a thousand subscribers, in June 2019, his subscribers increased to 100,000. He has collaborated with MiawAug in the game [[Secret Neighbor]] and was also invited to appear on stage at the 2019 YouTube FanFest event. By June 2020 he had reached one million subscribers. He is also known for the acting he usually performs during or at the end of his live streams, as part of his unique persona. He said that the performance is a form of [[gimmick]] to entertain and hopefully attract more viewers. He also said that he spent millions on costumes and miscellaneous items.{{cite news |last=Syahdan |first=Oleh |date=4 February 2022 |title=Windah Basudara Reveals His Hard Work in Making Gimmicks to Entertain Fans |publisher=Dunia Games |agency=Telkomsel |url=https://duniagames.co.id/discover/article/demi-menghibur-bocil-windah-basudara-beberkan-kerja-kerasnya-bikin-drama/en |access-date=5 June 2022}}
                
                On 8 July 2021,{{cite instagram |user=teamrrq |postid=CP2p_cThzXx |title=Welcome our new brand ambassador, RRQ Windah |date=8 June 2021}} Windah Basudara was officially announced, via their [[Instagram]] account (@teamrrq), as the new [[brand ambassador]] for the RRQ e-Sport team.{{cite web |date=9 June 2021 |title=Team RRQ Umumkan Brand Ambassador Baru: Windah Basudara |language=id |trans-title=RRQ Team Announces New Brand Ambassador: Windah Basudara |url=https://teamrrq.com/news/team-rrq-umumkan-brand-ambassador-baru-windah-basudara |website=TeamRRQ.com |publisher=[[Rex Regum Qeon]] |access-date=5 June 2022}}{{cite news |last=Yuliasari |first=Esih |date=9 June 2021 |title=Windah Basudara Resmi Berseragam RRQ |publisher=Biem.co |url=https://www.biem.co/read/2021/06/09/80391/windah-basudara-resmi-berseragam-rrq/ |access-date=5 June 2022}}
                
                In addition to his YouTube success, Windah has also contributed to fundraising events. One of the most memorable was a 24-hour livestream, where he managed to collect funds of IDR 50 million which he donated towards helping to deal with COVID-19.

                On 18 November 2021 at 18.30 [[UTC+07:00|GMT+7]], [[GTV (Indonesian TV network)|GTV]] presented the ''Indonesian Esports Awards 2021''. The Awards honoured the best Indonesian Esport players and YouTubers by Awarding the [[nominees]] based on the results of a public vote. The Indonesian Esports Awards 2021 was conducted by means of an [[online survey]], due to spread of the [[COVID-19 pandemic in Indonesia|coronavirus]].{{cite news |last=Aryani|first=Dwi|title=FOR YOULengkap!                 The ''Dunia Games Awards 2021'' was held 23–24 December 2021 and presented by [[:id:Dunia Games|Dunia Games]] and [[Telkomsel]]. The awards announcement was made virtually through [[Maxstream|MAXstream]], as well as Dunia Games and Telkomsel's [[Youtube channel|YouTube channels]]. There were 17 categories and winners in DGA 2021. Those categories were presented for gamers, professional esports athletes, content creators, esports organisers, local game publishers and developers, celebrity gamers, and esports fan clubs. The winner of the 2021 Dunia Games Award was determined by public vote held from 1–14 December 2021.{{cite news |title=Selamat, Ini Para Pemenang Dunia Games Awards 2021 Telkomsel |url=https://inet.detik.com/telecommunication/d-5869398/selamat-ini-para-pemenang-dunia-games-awards-2021-telkomsel |access-date=4 January 2022 |agency=[[detik.com]] |publisher=[[:id:detik Network|DetikInet]] |date=24 December 2021}}{{cite news |title=Telkomsel Umumkan Pemenang Dunia Games Awards 2021 |url=https://biz.kompas.com/read/2021/12/24/181841928/telkomsel-umumkan-pemenang-dunia-games-awards-2021 |access-date=4 January 2022 |agency=[[:id:Kompas.com|Kompas.com]] |publisher=[[Kompas]] |date=24 December 2021}} Windah Basudara won the award for ''Gaming Content Creator of the Year''.{{cite news |title=Inilah Daftar Pemenang Dunia Games Awards 2021, Windah Basudara Jadi Creator of the Year! |url=https://berita.yodu.id/pemenang-dunia-games-awards-windah-basudara/ |access-date=4 January 2022 |publisher=Yodi.id}}
